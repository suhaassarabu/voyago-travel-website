Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package? +
Fill in our Contact page booking form or call us directly. Our team will confirm availability and send a detailed itinerary within 24 hours.
Are flights included in the package price? +
Domestic packages typically exclude flights unless stated. All international packages include round-trip economy flights from Delhi.
Can I customise an itinerary? +
Absolutely! All packages can be personalised. Mention your preferences — dates, group size, budget — in the booking form's message field.
What is the cancellation policy? +
Cancellations made 15+ days before departure receive a full refund. Within 15 days, a 25% fee applies. Our team will guide you through any changes.
